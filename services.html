Smart Review Routing

Happy clients go to Google. Unhappy clients come to you. No more sending a blanket "leave us a 5-star review" link to someone who just had a bad experience — something that's happened to nearly every practice we've talked to.

Sentiment is assessed before anyone gets routed anywhere. Clients who are likely to leave a negative review get a private channel to share feedback with you directly. Everyone else gets the Google link.
