FIRST THINGS FIRST

WHAT'S A MED SPA?

A business that does skin treatments — Botox, facials, laser work. Picture a doctor's office crossed with a beauty salon. Visits run $200 to $800, and good customers come back every few months.

THEIR PROBLEM

NOBODY'S MINDING THE OFFICE

The owner is usually busy doing the treatments themselves. So phone messages go unanswered, regulars quietly drift away, and money slips out the door — not because the business is bad, but because nobody has time for the paperwork side.

OUR PRODUCT

A TIRELESS OFFICE ASSISTANT

We build a smart computer assistant for these businesses. It quietly handles the boring office work — day and night, weekends included, without ever being asked twice.

Think of the best front-desk employee you've ever met. Now imagine they never sleep, never forget a name, and never call in sick.

EXAMPLE ONE

BRINGING CUSTOMERS BACK

The assistant reads the appointment book, spots people who haven't visited in months, and sends a friendly "we'd love to see you again" note. Some of them book. Every one who does is real money the spa would have missed.

EXAMPLE TWO

ANSWERING AT MIDNIGHT

Someone texts the spa at 11pm: "How much is Botox?" Instead of waiting until morning — and maybe texting a competitor instead — they get an instant, friendly answer and an offer to book. The owner wakes up to a fuller calendar.

EXAMPLE THREE

COLLECTING GOLD STARS

After a good visit, the assistant politely asks the customer to leave a Google review. More five-star reviews means more new people find the spa when they search. It adds up fast.
