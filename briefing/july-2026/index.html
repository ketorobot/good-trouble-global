02 · Retrospective

LAST 30 DAYS

Good Trouble went from paperwork to a real operating company — live site, full infrastructure, business cards, flyers, pipeline — in about thirty days.

Win

Company Setup

LLC, EIN, business banking, Google Workspace, dedicated phone lines, brand system, business cards, flyers. The full corporate setup.

Win

The Tech Stack Build

Getting goodtroubleglobal.com live meant standing up the entire stack: domain acquisition and DNS zone configuration, SSL, a CDN-backed deploy pipeline with digest-based file manifests, a version-controlled repository with branch protection, SPF/DKIM/DMARC email authentication, GA4 analytics, a multi-model AI content pipeline wired across four provider APIs, and automated scripts running daily status reports.

Win

The Platform Demo

The flagship interactive demo is live — a working walkthrough of the platform running a med spa's back end: campaigns, front desk coverage, client follow-up, reviews, memberships, content. Client-specific versions built for warm targets.

Hurdle

The Manifest Wipe

The deploy pipeline deletes any page not included in the file manifest it's handed. One incomplete manifest went out and live pages disappeared from production. Cost a couple days. A hard deploy checklist is in place now.

Hurdle

Bad Timing on the First Outreach

The first ten personalized emails went to our ten best Tampa prospects right when the site was down. They clicked, nothing loaded. All ten are marked REVISIT — re-engaging them is week one of the plan. Pre-send site checks are now mandatory.

Win

Ten Personalized Demo Sites

Each of the top ten Tampa prospects got their own demo — their logo, their brand, their own URL, the platform running their business. Ten more are built and staged for the next batch.

Win

Walk-Ins Started

Started walking into Tampa med spas with the demo on a tablet. Quick chats at the front desk, a few real conversations with owners. The language they use fed straight back into the outreach copy.

Win

Three Case Studies

Three branded case studies live on the site — conservative modeled numbers with clear disclaimers, built so real client data drops straight in.

Hurdle

A Thousand Small Fires

The repo drifted out of sync with the live site, logo scrapers failed silently mid-build, an API endpoint 404'd mid-run. Nothing fatal, just lost hours. Most of it is written protocol now.

Win

The Outreach Arsenal

A 44-prospect enriched Tampa database, six cold email angles developed across two rounds, and Miami market research done on Augie's end.

Win

The Operating System

Internal founders' hub with automated daily 7am status emails to both of us, deploy rules, edit protocols, verification checklists.
