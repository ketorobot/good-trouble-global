About Good Trouble

We know this industry.
We built from inside it.

This isn't a software agency that pivoted to med spas last year. Alex spent a decade working directly inside the aesthetics industry before any of this existed. That's the difference.

After ten years running digital marketing for med spas — paid search, paid social, email, and retention strategy — the pattern was impossible to ignore. Every practice had the same three problems. A front desk that couldn't keep up with volume. A CRM full of lapsed clients nobody was contacting. And no-show rates quietly bleeding 15–25% of scheduled revenue every month.

The industry wasn't short on tools. It was short on execution. Every practice already had a booking platform, an email system, some version of a CRM — and almost none of it was actually connected, automated, or producing consistent results. The money was already there. It just wasn't being captured.

Good Trouble exists to close that gap. Not with a tool handoff, not with a 90-page onboarding doc, but with a team that runs the back end for you — the same way you'd want an operations hire to run it, except we're purpose-built for this specific industry and available on a subscription.
